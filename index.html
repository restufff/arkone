Kamen Rider ARK ONE

Villain / Anti-Hero

Despite being destroyed, Ark's will is not eliminated as long have malice exists in humans and/or Humagears. This point is proven by Horobi who claims that "as long have humans foster the essence of evil in their hearts, Ark will be revived" and by Azu. Aruto Hiden, who witnessed Izu's murder caused by Horobi, fell into despair, grief, sadness and anger, which is all of the negative emotions Ark needs to "resurrect". Using this emotions, he transformed into Ark-One, despite the fact that Thouser, Vulcan and Valkyrie all thought that Ark was possessing Zero-One, while it was the exact reverse.
